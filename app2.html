Trackr: A project management application

This app is all about Project Management and streamlining the process. Trackr allows you to track projects with ease. You can assign team members to a board, create alerts, customize alerts to your choosing, create notes, and view your project statistics via your dashboard.

The main user this app is designed for is a project or product manager who needs to keep their tasks in order and stay on top of upcoming or ongoing projects.
